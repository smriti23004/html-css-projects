Gulab Jamun

ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup and remove from the fire. Add cardamom and keep aside to cool
  2. In a mixing bowl, add the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough. Do not knead too much.
  3. Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round Gulab Jamuns. Fry in the oil at a very low flame till golden bro
  4. Add the fried Gulab Jamuns to the prepared sugar syrup. Once all the Gulab Jamuns are in the sugar syrup, bring it to a boil and remove from the flame.
  5. Serve warm

Milk Barfi

ingredients

  • Sugar
  • ghee
  • milk
  • pistachios

Recipe

  1. Firstly, in a bowl take 2½ cup milk powder, ¾ cup sugar and 1 cup milk
  2. Whisk and mix well until everything is well combined.Transfer the mixture into large kadai. recommend using nonstick pan to prevent from sticking
  3. Keep stirring on low flame until the mixture thickens.
  4. The mixture will hold the shape and starts to separate the pan after 10 minutes.
  5. Do not overcook, as the burfi will turn hard. and if the mixture is undercooked then it turns chewy
  6. Transfer the burfi dough into the tray lined with baking paper. Press gently, making sure its leveled up
  7. Top with few chopped pistachios and press gently. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour or until it sets completely
  8. Finally, milk burfi recipe tastes great for a week when stored in an airtight container
